Medtime

Client: Study case
Role: UX Designer, UI Designer, Research

Project

In the MediTime project, I embarked on an exciting creative journey, starting with a vision to create a mobile app that revolutionizes medication management. The primary goal of this project was to deliver an exceptional experience, simplifying the process of setting personalized alarms for multiple medicines, while also providing convenient suggestions for purchasing medications. With a strong focus on user-centric design, I aimed to empower users like Paolo and Ana to effortlessly manage their medication routines, making it a seamless and enjoyable process for their very first time using the app.

Goals

  • Simplify medication management with an intuitive app, allowing users to set personalized alarms for different medicines effortlessly.
  • Enhance organization by introducing dedicated sections for recurrent and regular medicines, along with customizable categories for personalized medication management.
  • Provide smart recommendations for medication scheduling and pharmacy suggestions, offering users convenience and support in managing their prescriptions effectively.

Empathizing with the user

Our personas, Paolo and Ana, represented diverse user needs and expectations. As we embarked on designing the ideal mobile app, their insights and preferences guided us towards creating a truly user-centric experience that catered to the unique requirements of both tech-savvy professionals and caring caregivers alike.

Primary persona

"Having to remember all the medicines to take is complicated!"

From a young age, Paolo has been battling various respiratory diseases, enduring long and challenging treatment regimens. Despite the hardships, like any young adult, he yearns to savor life's moments and relish in its joys. However, amidst his pursuits of enjoyment, there are instances when Paolo forgets to take his crucial medications. The constant struggle to strike a balance between living a fulfilling life and adhering to his treatments weighs heavily on him, prompting the need for a solution that can empower him to lead a healthier and more carefree life without compromising on his well-being.

Goals

A need a tool to help me remember the medicines I need to take.

Frustrations

The other apps I have tried, are too complicated to set any reminder.

Secondary persona

"Having busy schedules that is constantly alternating is no fun"

As a dedicated Gym Trainer, I face the occasional complexities of my ever-changing schedule, which varies from week to week. Balancing my commitments can be quite challenging, especially when it comes to ensuring I take my essential supplements and pre-training regimen consistently. The nature of my profession demands unwavering focus and energy, making it crucial for me to find effective ways to remember and adhere to my supplement routine amidst the constant hustle and bustle. Finding a reliable solution to effortlessly integrate these essential elements into my busy routine would be a game-changer, allowing me to perform at my best and achieve my fitness goals with greater ease.

Goals

I need a tool to easily have many schedules of medicine treatments, so I can switch between them.

Frustrations

Using the current apps, I have to create new schedules every week.

First round of User Testing with Lo-Fi Prototypes

During this crucial phase of app development, we conducted user testing with Lo-Fi prototypes to gather valuable insights and feedback from our personas, Alex and Sarah. Observing their interactions and navigation patterns, we assessed the initial design concepts' usability and identified potential areas for improvement. The candid feedback provided us with valuable insights into their pain points and preferences, guiding us to refine the user interface and create a more user-centric experience. Armed with this knowledge, we will now proceed to develop more polished Hi-Fi prototypes, ensuring that our commitment to a seamless and delightful user experience remains at the forefront of our design process.

Refining User Experience

After conducting the first tests, I meticulously separated and analyzed crucial improvements required for the screens. These valuable insights allowed me to identify specific areas that needed refinement in order to enhance the overall user experience. By addressing these key findings, I was confident that subsequent design iterations would result in a more user-friendly and intuitive interface. With the users' needs and preferences at the forefront, these crucial improvements paved the way for a seamless and delightful user experience, ensuring the app met and exceeded the expectations of our target audience.

Empowering User-Centric Experience: MediTime's Hi-Fi Prototypes

Building upon the insights gleaned from comprehensive user testing with our Lo-Fi prototypes, the MediTime app underwent a transformative evolution with the creation of its Hi-Fi prototypes. Armed with a deep understanding of user needs and preferences, we meticulously updated the Lo-Fi designs, addressing crucial pain points and streamlining the user interface. The Hi-Fi prototypes showcased a visually stunning and intuitive app that ensured seamless medication management for users like Paolo and Ana. With improved navigational flow, personalized medication alarms, and enhanced pharmacy suggestion features, the Hi-Fi prototypes embodied a truly user-centric experience. The incorporation of valuable insights not only fine-tuned the app's functionality but also amplified its visual appeal, guaranteeing a delightful journey for users as they interacted with MediTime.

Medication Management: Streamlined Sections and Customizable Categories

In an effort to optimize the medication management experience for users, I implemented two significant improvements in the MediTime app. Firstly, I introduced a dedicated section exclusively for recurrent and regular medicines. This thoughtful addition enables users to easily differentiate and manage medications that require daily or frequent administration, streamlining their medication routines and ensuring important doses are never missed. Secondly, I expanded the app's capabilities by incorporating the option to add customizable categories and personalized sections for medicines. This empowering feature allows users to organize their medications based on their preferences, facilitating easier access and effective management of various types of medicines.

Enhanced Medication Management: Streamlined Scheduling

I implemented an "Add More" option under the time picker, providing users with the convenience of effortlessly adding multiple time slots for their medication reminders. This feature streamlines the process of setting alarms for recurrent and regular medicines, ensuring users can easily manage their complex medication schedules. Secondly, I incorporated a smart feature that suggests common usage patterns, such as "3 times per day," empowering users with pre-defined options that align with their prescribed dosage. This addition simplifies the task of setting reminders, offering users helpful recommendations and reducing the effort required to schedule their medications accurately.

Enhanced Convenience: Smart Suggestions for Medication Purchases

This new feature provides users with convenience and ease by offering smart recommendations for nearby pharmacies where they can purchase their prescribed medications. By integrating this functionality, I aimed to address the common challenge of finding the right pharmacy and getting the best prices for their medicines. With this enhancement, MediTime not only becomes a comprehensive medication management tool but also a valuable resource that empowers users to make informed decisions about their healthcare needs. The addition of this feature ensures a seamless and hassle-free experience for users, making MediTime a truly indispensable companion for all their medication requirements.

Final Considerations

  • User-Centric Design: Throughout the development process, the primary focus has been on creating a user-friendly and intuitive experience for individuals like Alex and Sarah. By actively incorporating user feedback and insights, MediTime ensures that it caters to the unique needs and preferences of its users.
  • Simplifying Medication Management: The core objective of MediTime is to simplify the complex process of medication management. With personalized alarms, dedicated sections, and customizable categories, the app streamlines medication routines, reducing the likelihood of missed doses and promoting better adherence to prescribed regimens.
  • Smart Recommendations: The inclusion of smart recommendations, such as suggested usage patterns and nearby pharmacy suggestions, further enhances the user experience, making it easier for users to manage their medications efficiently and conveniently.
  • Continuous Improvement: MediTime is a dynamic and evolving project, with room for continuous improvement and updates. As new insights emerge and technologies advance, the app will continue to evolve to meet the ever-changing needs of its users.
  • Empowering Health and Well-Being: Ultimately, MediTime aims to empower users in taking charge of their health and well-being. By offering an all-in-one solution for medication management, the app seeks to alleviate the burden of scheduling and organizing medications, allowing users to focus on what matters most - their health and quality of life.

In conclusion, this redesign project stands as a testament to the fusion of technological innovation and user-centric design. By amplifying AI capabilities and adopting a minimalistic approach, the interface underwent a transformation aimed at enhancing user engagement and accessibility. The streamlined design, simplified interactions, and strategic messaging collectively contribute to a more compelling user experience. As the digital landscape evolves, this project serves as a foundation for continuous improvement, reinforcing the commitment to adaptability, user-centricity, and ongoing refinement to meet evolving user needs.
